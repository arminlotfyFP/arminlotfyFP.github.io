I am an expert in digital electrical engineering with a strong focus on Artificial Intelligence, specializing in machine learning, deep learning, and reinforcement learning. My work centers on leveraging AI-driven models for data-driven prediction, decision-making, and system optimization. This includes developing intelligent solutions for energy management in electric vehicles—a critical area for the future of sustainable transportation.

I hold an M.Sc. in Digital Electrical Engineering from Iran University of Science and Technology and am currently pursuing a Ph.D. at Carleton University as part of the Intelligent Robotic and Energy Systems Research Group (IRES).

My research is dedicated to advancing the application of deep learning and reinforcement learning techniques to energy management systems in electric vehicles. I have extensive experience with Python and its libraries, including TensorFlow, Keras, Scikit-learn, NumPy, and Pandas, which I use to design, train, and implement sophisticated AI algorithms. By integrating these tools with MATLAB, I create cutting-edge solutions that demonstrate the transformative potential of AI in addressing complex real-world challenges.
